Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 28, 2026 · Save-O ("we", "us", "our")

Save-O is a personal finance tracking application. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information — name, email address, and a hashed password when you create an account.
  • Financial data — transaction descriptions, amounts, categories, and dates you enter manually or via voice.
  • Voice recordings — when you use the voice-entry feature, a short audio clip is sent to our server, transcribed using OpenAI Whisper, and immediately deleted after processing. We do not store raw audio.
  • Project/investment data — project names, partner emails, ownership percentages, and ledger entries for shared ventures.
  • Device & usage data — we may collect anonymous crash logs and performance metrics via Expo's built-in telemetry (no advertising identifiers are used).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and personalise the Save-O service (dashboards, analytics, AI assistant).
  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To improve the accuracy of voice transcription and AI recommendations.
  • To send transactional emails (e.g., password reset). We do not send marketing emails.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase (database hosting) — your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase Inc. with encryption at rest.
  • OpenAI — voice transcripts and AI assistant queries are processed via OpenAI's API. OpenAI's data usage policy applies to those API calls.
  • Railway — our backend API is hosted on Railway Inc.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

4. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing support@saveo.app.

5. Security

Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. All API communication uses HTTPS/TLS. Database connections use SSL. We follow industry-standard practices but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Save-O is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at support@saveo.app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at support@saveo.app.
